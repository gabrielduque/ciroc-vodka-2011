Jean-Sebastian Robiquet

Bio Photos

Jean-Sebastian Robiquet's family roots in the renowned French wine region Sud-Quest reaches back to the 17th century and his family is known for being one of the very first family-owned business in the Cognac trade.

Jean-Sebastian earned his oenology diploma in 1988 and a degree in Master in Lay, Economics and Management of the Wine Industry in 1990. Recognized by the Great council of Bordeaux wines as 'Commandeur of Bordeaux,' in acknowledgement of his strong demand for high quality and high quality, honest products.
