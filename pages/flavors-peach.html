SNAP FROST RED BERRY COCONUT PEACH

Like original CÎROC® vodka, CÎROC Peach is made using fine French grapes that are cold fermented (not unlike fine wines) and distilled five times - the fifth distillation in a pot still, a process normally reserved for creation of fine whiskey or brandy.
